About SMCC

SMCC (Sample Multi-Community College) is dedicated to providing quality education and training to our students. Our mission is to empower individuals through knowledge and skills.

We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of our diverse student body, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to succeed in their chosen field.

Vision

Saint Michael envisions to be a University by 2035, upholding spiritual formation and excellence in teaching, service, and research.

Mission

SMCC shall provide spiritual formation and a learning culture that will ensure students with excellent and rewarding learning experiences that transform lives, abound spirituality, develop skills, and prepare future leaders.


SMCC shall engage in dynamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary researches that are publishable to advance and achieve institutional initiatives.


SMCC shall commit to serving diverse and local communities in fostering innovations through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.


Goals

Uphold Culture of Excellence in the Areas of Spiritual Formation, Instruction, Research, and Extension, thus Produce Graduates that are Globally Competent, Spiritually Embodied, and Socially Responsible.
